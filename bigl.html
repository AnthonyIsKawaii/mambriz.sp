What's new?

  • Infusions! (Sorta almost kinda not really)
    • Infusions are even more of a bitch than I thought.
    • "Most" infusions should work WITHOUT REINFORCEMENT.
    • As soon as you introduce reinforcement, things get pretty wacky.
    • It's going to take a lot of trial and error to get reinforced infusions working, so please bear with me while I piece through the spaghetti.
    • Everything that was working before """"should"""" still be working now.
  • There's a total value for each damage type now.
    • You might notice that this value is one too large.
    • The reason for this being that damage values actually have decimal places that aren't displayed, both in game and in this calculator.
    • The displayed total here is the "true" total, including the unseen decimals.

What this does:

  • Calculates the displayed AR for almost every melee weapon in the game.

What this does not (AKA todo list):

    • Ranged weapons might use the same formula, but I haven't tested them.
  • Look pretty on smaller sized screens. (Really only looks alright on 1920x1080)
